DISCOVERY

The Discovery Seminar is a unique, interactive, lecture and small group based experiential program. Participants examine their attitudes and belief systems in an interactive and reflective way with others. The goal of Discovery is to equip participants for radical transformation so that they can live the full and productive life that aligns with their vision.
